Animation Videos

As a final for my Jewish Prague class, we had to make an art project that represented the history. I chose to do an animation video in the style of "It's a Small World".

These are two animations I put together. I made these as part of a project for my Theme Parks and Attractions class. We had to create a theme park with a theme of our choosing. We did Doctor Who. The first video is a concept for a ride, "Time Wars" where one side is The Doctor and the other side is Daleks. The second video is an idea for a DIY TARDIS. The zoom in is not good. I know.

This started out as a flip book. It took a lot of time to actually make it work because Pencil2D sucks. It was drawn in that but edited and exported through iMovie.
