About Callie Deas

Callie Deas is a senior computer science major with a math minor at Belmont University in Nashville, TN. She grew up in Columbus, Ohio, but moved to Nashville originally to study audio engineering. Switching to computer science sophomore year, she found a major she enjoyed for its creative problem solving, versitality, and job market. Callie currently works for event services at Belmont in many different positions including a leadership position of Ops Lead. Though she enjoys her job, she is excited to start her career in computer science and eventually would like to be a project manager. She is also a strong advocate for women in computer science and runs a Lean In Circle at school along with being vice president of the math and computer science club. Junior year, Callie studied abroad in Prague and traveled all over Europe. More about that can be found in her blogs.
